Who Are We?

Fal

From the lands of Here Be Dragons, or more simply Western Mass, Fal is your genderfluid member of Nightlite. They tend to be a bit of smartass which involve improv lines to games and swear like a motherfucking sailor. Fal will generally play any kind of game and is often the comic relief or the voice of (sometimes purposefully misguided) reason.

Legend

Legend is a Southern New Hampshire resident who can be slightly crazy. She don't like horror games, but can put up with some of them to a degree. Although she has a rather calm demeanor, she can say and do abnormal or chaotic things when put in a certain situation, whether voicing a character or in general. Legend is usually used for either in game sound effects, especially for sick people (to Fal's dismay).
